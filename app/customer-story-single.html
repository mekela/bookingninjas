Established in 1952, Hospitality Financial and Technology Professionals (HFTP®) has been a voice for the financial and technology segments of the hospitality industry, providing solutions to the challenges of stakeholders in the industry.

HFTP is an Authoritative Source in Hospitality Finance and Technology which actively supports and develops projects and resources to make them widely available to the hospitality industry. HFTP remains true to its core purpose: To lead and advance the hospitality profession by providing a forum for continuous learning and knowledge sharing. A big role HFTP plays in the hospitality industry is bringing together and organizing its expert networks, research, certification programs, information resources and conferences/events such as HITEC. The owner of the world's only hospitality-specific search engine, PineappleSearch.com®, the organization is dedicated to providing value to the entire hospitality industry worldwide, running its operations from its headquarters in Austin, Texas, USA, with offices in the United Kingdom, Hong Kong, Dubai, and the Netherlands.

How Booking Ninjas came in

HFTP® constantly searches for ways to improve the lives of its members and stakeholders, including exposing them to various important information, introducing systems that could be of help, providing top-level research, as well as organize events and conferences.

As a result, there is an increasing need for:

  • Making hospitality business easier for members and stakeholders,
  • Handling of events/conference management,
  • Monitoring email marketing,
  • Improving engagement with stakeholders,
  • Management of members and stakeholders in a single portal, and
  • A single platform to take care of everything.

Such needs make them leverage digital solutions, including the Booking Ninjas platform.

The Booking Ninjas has proved a flexible platform for the organization as it makes autonomous handling of their operations easier than ever. The ability of Booking Ninjas to integrate other Salesforce apps on a single platform makes it an indispensable tool towards achieving its main purpose.

David Harroch, CEO of Booking Ninjas noted:

“There is a significant amount of similarities between Booking Ninjas and HFTP, in that their major drive is to constantly improve the lives and businesses of their stakeholders, and this partnership has helped to reinstate the purpose in the two platforms”.
