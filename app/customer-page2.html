An all-digital hotel might sound like a lot of work at first. Or maybe too complex to achieve in the first place. Here’s how CTY Books is pushing through that barrier, taking the lead, and getting results.
David Harroch Founder/CEO, Booking Ninjas
For CTY Books, handling a hotel shouldn’t be limited to manual operations alone. A digital inclusion to their core hotel management process is crucial, as it enables the hotel to get more tasks done faster and more efficiently.

Moreover, there’s much more work to be done rather than handing tasks that could be better carried out by a property management system, hence the need for a digital solution that can provide the core services needed for the smooth running of a hotel.

How Booking Ninjas came in

The need explained above could be solved by getting individual tools from the AppExchange, or other digital tools store. However, it is of paramount importance to have all your business being run from a centralized application, with just one platform. That’s where Booking Ninjas come in.

The cloud-based Booking Ninjas platform constitutes a highly specialized solution to hotels intending to run their operations digitally. It offered features that enable CTY Books to perform more efficiently while allowing them integrating several tools for the quality digital management of the hotel.
