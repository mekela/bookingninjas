How Norwalk Inn Eliminated Slow Service

And got the tools to realize their growth potential at the same time.

Norwalk Inn and Conference center is less than an hour’s drive from New York City. They offer 116 rooms which include 10 luxury suites. The easy access to both leisure and business hubs makes them quite popular. And a popular inn with this amount of rooms needs a smooth system to function at 100%.

They were still using an old legacy property management system(PMS) and point of sale(PoS) system. It didn’t quite match their thirst for growth or efficiency - information was scattered all over the place. For example, most days started off great. It’s quiet and the staff have some time to prep everything. But then the rush starts. And, thanks to the slow PoS system, the lines grow. Frustrated customers fill the lobby and some even start to leave.

Not only that, but when information is required to make split decisions, they have to wait for up to 3 weeks for the information to be available. Clearly, this isn’t working.
